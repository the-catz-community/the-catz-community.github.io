Who We Are

The CATZ clan value's friendships, loyalty, good times, and cats. If you like the sound of that, then the CATZ community is the purrfect fit for you! We believe in building a fun stable gaming environment where respect, kindness and acceptance always come first.

Whether you play on PC or Console, our goal stays the same: creating an unforgettable atmosphere, capturing amazing memories, and standing by our crew members across every generation of gaming.

Community Rules

These rules apply to everyone in our community, regardless of platform (PC, PS5, Xbox).

01

Respect & Safety

  • Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, bullying, sexism, racism, homophobia, or hate speech.
  • No political or religious debates or topics.
  • If something makes you feel unsafe, report it to staff immediately.
02

Malicious Content

  • No viruses, IP grabbers, malware, or harmful links. Instant Permanent Ban
  • No doxxing, threats, violence, DDoSing, or leaking private details. Instant Permanent Ban
  • No posting someone’s personal info without permission. Instant Permanent Ban
03

Content Restrictions

  • No age-restricted, obscene, or graphic content (exception: designated 18+ channels).
  • No nudity, sexual content, gore, hard violence, or disturbing imagery.
  • Absolutely no content involving minors (<18). Instant Ban
  • No terrorism, animal abuse, or prohibited material.
04

Messaging & Chat

  • No spam, flooding, or excessive pings.
  • Do not ping @everyone, staff, or specific users without a valid reason.
05

Profiles & Names

  • No offensive, inappropriate, sexual, racist, or hateful nicknames or profile pictures.
  • No blank nicknames, blank profile pictures, or unreadable glitch/Unicode names.
06

Honesty & Identity

  • No fake or stolen photos, names, or personal information.
  • No deceiving staff or crew members.
  • Do not reveal private DMs without consent unless actively reporting to staff.
